Computer Labs

This is where Software Development students typically work on their programming projects. The labs may also be utilized for other courses such as Statistics, which may require software such as Excel.


MMA Labs

Here is where all of our MMA students do their work. Classrooms

iACADEMY offers traditional classrooms to provide a more conventional learning environment and hands-on learning opportunities. These classrooms may also serve as backup rooms.

Science Labs

Here is where the students do their experiments for science class. In these labs, students can conduct hands-on experiments and apply scientific principles to various fields of study such as biology, chemistry, physics, and geology. The labs are equipped with specialized tools and equipment, including microscopes, test tubes, Bunsen burners, balances, and other laboratory apparatus.


MMA Labs

There are also MMA Labs here on the 10th floor. These labs are equipped with specialized software and hardware such as Adobe Photoshop, Illustrator, Premiere, Final Cut Pro, Autodesk Maya, Unity, graphics tablets, scanners, and cameras to support students' creative processes in producing multimedia projects.

Auditorium

Here is where iACADEMY hold their important events.


Exhibit Room

Here is where iACADEMY hold their exhibits. This room can be used to demonstrate mastery of course material, and to display student artwork and designs. Ultimately, exhibit rooms celebrate the accomplishments of students and provide recognition for their hard work and dedication.

PE rooms

Here is where iACADEMY hold discussions during PE class.


Gym

The gym is used for physical education classes, sports practices and games, and individual exercise and fitness activities. The gym is also used for sports practices and games, providing a space for students to train and compete in various sports such as basketball, volleyball, and soccer.

Tracks Field

Here is where iACADEMY runners run their laps.


Library

The libraries in iACADEMY are used primarily as resource centers for students and faculty members. These libraries are equipped with various types of materials such as books, periodicals, e-books, and databases to support research and academic learning.
