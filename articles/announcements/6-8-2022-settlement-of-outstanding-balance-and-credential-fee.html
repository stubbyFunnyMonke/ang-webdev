Settlement of Outstanding Balance and Credential Fee

June 08, 2022

Please be reminded of the payment deadlines for Outstanding balance and Credential fee - on or before June 30, 2022 (end of business 5pm).

Please be advised that all proofs of payment should be sent to collegefinance@iacademy.edu.ph for validation and crediting to the respective student’s account. Official Receipts (OR) will be dated on the day proof of payment is received and successfully validated.

For Direct Bank Deposit, kindly write the student ID number and the student’s full name on the deposit slip then send a clear copy of the whole deposit slip. Please keep the original slip and present to the cashier when claiming the original copy of the issued OR.

For Fund Transfer and Debit/Credit Card Internet Payment Gateway (IPG), you can send the email confirmation or the transfer completion which shows the following:

Transaction Date & Time
Transaction Amount
Reference Number
Name and Account number of receiver (last four digits will do)
Note : All 3rd Term 2021-2022 Graduating Students end term clearance will only be signed upon confirmation of payment of deficiencies.
