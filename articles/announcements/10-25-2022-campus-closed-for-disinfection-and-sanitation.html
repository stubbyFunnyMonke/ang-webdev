ADVISORY: iACADEMY Nexus Campus Closed on October 26 for Disinfection and Sanitation

October 25, 2022

Dear Game Changers,

Due to the cluster of COVID-19 cases confirmed in iACADEMY today, October 25, 2022, the iACADEMY Nexus Campus will be closed tomorrow, October 26, 2022, for a thorough disinfection and sanitation.

Onsite classes for SHS and COLLEGE shall be suspended and moved ONLINE on October 26, 2022.

Onsite classes for COLLEGE will resume on Thursday, October 27, 2022, while onsite classes for SENIOR HIGH SCHOOL will resume on Thursday, November 3, 2022.

Rest assured that iACADEMY will continue to implement safety measures and protocols to ensure the well-being and safety of our students and staff.

Stay safe and healthy, Game Changers!

For further inquiries, email us at studentsupport@iacademy.edu.ph.
